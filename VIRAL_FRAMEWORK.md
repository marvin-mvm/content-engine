## Caption & Subtitle Rules (Algorithm-Confirmed)

1. **Always burn subtitles** — most IG/TikTok consumption is silent
2. **Large, high-contrast captions** — must be readable on a moving escalator (glance test)
3. **CTA in captions**: "Save this", "Follow for more", "Comment if you agree"
4. **Hook in caption matches hook in video** — reinforces the message
5. **First line of caption = hook** — Instagram shows 1–2 lines before "more" cutoff

### Caption Formula:
```
[HOOK — scroll-stopping first line]

[Value delivery — 2-3 lines of actual info]

[Acme Labs positioning]

[CTA — "Save this. Follow for daily longevity science."]

#peptides #longevity #biohacking #acmelabs #performancescience
```

## Video Structure for Maximum Retention

```
0–2s   HOOK — bold text overlay + spoken hook + visual pattern interrupt
2–5s   PROOF — stat, study reference, or credibility signal
5–8s   VALUE — the actual content (mechanism, science, claim)
8–10s  CTA  — "Follow Acme Labs" or "For research use only · acmehealth.co"
```

**Retention benchmark:** Must keep >50% of viewers past 3 seconds. If not, the hook failed.

---

## What NOT to Do (Anti-patterns)

- ❌ Generic "Check out our product" CTAs — no one shares these
- ❌ Text-heavy educational carousels as the only format — mix in video
- ❌ Same format every video — vary between talking-head, B-roll, stat drops
- ❌ Posting without a hook in the first frame
- ❌ Captions too small to read on mobile without zooming
- ❌ Long intros — get to the point in 1.5 seconds
